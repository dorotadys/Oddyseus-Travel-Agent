Coronavirus travel restrictions

As per new travel restructions from 10 July 2020 travellers will not be required to self-isolatewhen they arrive in England, if they:

  • are travelling or returning from one of the countries with travel corridor exemption;
  • have not been to or stopped in a country that’s not on the travel corridors exemption list in the previous 14 days.

This applies to all travel to England, by train, ferry, coach, air or any other route.

Those arriving from the countries that are not on the travel corridors exemption list will have to self-isolate until 14 days have passed since you left that country.

Thomas Hartley Retirement

Thomas Hartley, one of the funding directors of Oddyseus announced his returement from the role of Chef Financial Officer. He has funded Occyseus in 2005 along and worked in the company since then.

Martin Lawrence, a chairman and chef executive officer: "I want to thank Tom for his many contributions over the course of his distinguished career at Oddyseus,” said: “As a highly valued member of our company’s executive leadership team, Tom played an important role in guiding our successful transformation as an independent exploration and production company. I wish Tom the very best in retirement.”
